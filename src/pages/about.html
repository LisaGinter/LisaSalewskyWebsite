Aside from my education and work experience, there's a lot more to know about me! I love Japan and had the amazing opportunity to travel the country for 6 months after my Abitur and then study abroad for a semester during my master's studies. Through living in Japan, I got to improve my Japanese skills, but I also learned so much more about the country, the culture and - most importantly - I met so many great people! Starting with small and easy costumes, I quickly learned more and more advanced techniques and reached a level where I can confidently sew daily clothes for myself.

First, we practiced in the basement of our homes, in our rooms, in different parks and just wherever possible. But a few years later, we started saving up and organized a room at a local kindergarden and AWO venue, bought large practice mirrors and got them installed. So, we finally had a place for practice. From there, we mostly danced and practiced for ourselves, but we also had a few performances and entered two contests.
